Embodiment.

Bolens mentions that "[...] language is an artefact employed to interact" and written form is only one of many variations of how language can be conveyed. This essay explores five different ways that text can be conveyed to a reader based on the five ways of embodiment introduced by Mark Johnson.

By clicking through the text, you will uncover deeper meanings and learn more about the cultural significance of each line. Get ready to embark on this poetic journey!
